Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

1. Never share your UPI PIN with anyone.
2. Do not scan QR codes to receive money.
3. Always verify the sender before accepting payment requests.
4. Avoid clicking on unknown or suspicious links.
5. Enable two-factor authentication on apps.
6. Use only official apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, or Paytm.
7. Regularly check your bank transaction history.
8. Do not respond to calls claiming to be bank officials asking for details.

UPI Safety Tips:
9. Remember: entering UPI PIN means you are sending money.
10. Reject unknown “collect requests” immediately.

Report Fraud:
• National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
• Helpline Number: 1930
